# Part 2 — Instruction Signs (201–231)

## Category Overview

- **Hebrew:** תמרורי הוריה
- **Shape:** Rectangular or custom shape (arrows, path signs)
- **Color:** Blue background with white or yellow symbols/arrows
- **Purpose:** Direct the driver to follow a specific route, direction, or movement
- **Key rule:** Yellow arrows on instruction signs indicate the permitted direction for public transport (bus/tram) at intersections and traffic lights

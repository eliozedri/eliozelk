# Part 6 — Information & Guidance Signs (601–640)

## Category Overview

- **Hebrew:** תמרורי מידע והכוונה
- **Purpose:** Provide information about destinations, distances, road numbers, parking, and special zones
- **Color scheme:**
  - **Blue** background: motorway (fast road) directions
  - **Green** background: other roads (non-urban)
  - **Brown** background: tourist/recreational destinations
  - **White** background: urban road directions (within city)
- **Supplementary signs:** 629 (annotation), 630 (distance), 631 (section length), 632 (after-turn direction) are attached below other signs to extend or modify their scope

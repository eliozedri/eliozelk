## Supplementary / Modifier Signs

These signs attach below other signs and modify their scope or extent:

| Modifier Sign | Modifies | How |
|--------------|---------|-----|
| 439 | Any restriction sign | Adds annotation text (time, day, vehicle type, distance); ends zone when placed at zone end |
| 629 | Any information sign | Adds annotation text to guidance signs |
| 630 | Any distance-linked sign | Shows distance to destination |
| 631 | Any length-linked sign | Shows length of road feature (tunnel, bridge, etc.) |
| 632 | Parking zone signs | Shows parking duration limit |

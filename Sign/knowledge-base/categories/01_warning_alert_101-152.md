# Part 1 — Warning & Alert Signs (101–152)

## Category Overview

- **Hebrew:** תמרורי הרהזאה ואזהרה
- **Shape:** Equilateral triangle, point upward
- **Color:** Red border, yellow/amber fill, black symbol inside
- **Purpose:** Warn the driver of a hazard or unusual condition on the road ahead
- **Illuminated variants:** Signs with prefix פ (e.g., פ150, פ151) are self-luminous (emit their own light)
- **Placement:** Generally on the right side of the road; over the road when above a specific lane

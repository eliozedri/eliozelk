# Sign Families

Grouped relationships between signs that share shape, function, or operational context.

## 4. Traffic Light Families

### 4a. Vehicle Signals
701 (red) → 702 (yellow) → 703 (green)
704 (straight arrow) / 705 (right arrow) / 706 (left arrow) — supplement or replace 703

### 4b. Tram / LRT Signals
708 (stop) → 709 (straight bar) / 710 (right arrow) / 711 (left arrow) → 712–717 (combined movement variants)

### 4c. Pedestrian Signals
718 (red standing figure) ↔ 719 (green walking figure)

### 4d. Bicycle Crossing Signals
720 (red bicycle) ↔ 721 (green bicycle)

### 4e. Overhead Lane Control
722 (open ↓) / 723 (closed ✕) / 724 (closing ✕ yellow) / 725 (merge →) / 726 (merge ←)
Illuminated equivalents: פ722–פ726

### 4f. Bicycle Intersection Phase Signals
727 (red) → 728 (yellow) → 729 (green)

## 5. Supplementary / Modifier Signs

These signs are always mounted **below** the primary sign they modify. They do not stand alone.

| Sign | Role | Can Modify |
|------|------|-----------|
| 439 | Text annotation (time, day, vehicle type) | Any restriction or instruction sign |
| 629 | Text annotation for guidance signs | Any information/guidance sign |
| 630 | Distance to destination | Direction and guidance signs |
| 631 | Length of road feature | Tunnel, bridge, or route signs |
| 632 | Maximum parking duration | Parking zone signs (432, 433) |

## 6. Railway Crossing Advance Warning Triad

Three signs placed at 300 m, 200 m, and 100 m ahead of a level crossing:

| Distance | Sign | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| 300 m | 130 | Three diagonal bars |
| 200 m | 131 | Two diagonal bars |
| 100 m | 132 | One diagonal bar |

Followed by one of:
- 133 (single-track crossing, no barrier)
- 134 (multi-track crossing, no barrier)
- 135 (crossing with barriers)

## 7. Work Zone Sign Families

### 7a. Structural Sequence
901 (entry warning) → 903 (speed limit) → 905/906 (lane closure / taper) → 917 or 914 (control) → 904 (end speed) → 902 (end work zone)

### 7b. Delineation / Channelling
933 (delineator panel) / פ933 (illuminated) / 934 (arrow board) / פ934 (illuminated) / 935 (closure barrier)

### 7c. Orange Equivalents of Standard Signs
Every work zone regulatory sign (903, 917, 919, 920–932) has an exact permanent-sign counterpart in Parts 1–4; the orange colour is the only distinguishing feature.

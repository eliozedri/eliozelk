# Part 4 — Prohibitions & Restrictions (401–441)

## Category Overview

- **Hebrew:** תמרורי איסורים ומגבלות
- **Shape:** Circle (prohibition) or rectangle (zone markers)
- **Color:** Red border on white background; zone-start/end signs are rectangular
- **Purpose:** Prohibit specific movements, vehicle types, or activities, or restrict speed, weight, height, width
- **Zone signs:** Many come in start/end pairs. The restriction applies from the start sign until the nearest intersection, or until the matching end sign, or until sign 434 (universal restriction end)
- **Illuminated variants:** Signs with פ prefix are self-luminous

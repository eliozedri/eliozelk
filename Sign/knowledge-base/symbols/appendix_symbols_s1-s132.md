# Appendix: Symbols (ס-1 — ס-132)

**Hebrew:** סמלים
**Symbol range:** ס-1 through ס-132 (with reserved/blank slots noted)
**Purpose:** Symbols (סמלים) are standardised pictograms and value markers that appear *within* the main signs throughout Parts 1–9. They are not independent signs; they give the signs their specific meaning.
**File naming:** `symbol_s001.png`, `symbol_s004.png`, etc. Reserved slots have no image file.

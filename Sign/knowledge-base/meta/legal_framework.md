## Sign Size Requirements (Section 4(d))

Minimum dimensions for standard sign types:

| Sign Shape | Dimension | Minimum Size |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Triangle (warning) | Side length | 600 mm |
| Circle (prohibition/instruction) | Diameter | 500 mm |
| Octagon (STOP — sign 302) | Blocking circle diameter | 650 mm |
| Rectangle (information) | Height | Per design specification |
| Road surface marking width | Line width | Per marking type specification |

Signs on high-speed roads (≥80 km/h) must use the larger size category specified in the relevant size table.

## Placement Rules (Section 4(a)(b)(c))

**Section 4(a) — Lateral position:**
- Signs are normally placed on the right side of the road, at the edge of the carriageway or shoulder.
- On divided roads or multi-lane roads, signs may additionally be placed on the left side or overhead.
- Overhead signs apply only to the lane(s) directly beneath them.

**Section 4(b) — Height:**
- Minimum clearance above the road surface: 600 mm (bottom of sign to road).
- In urban areas where pedestrians pass beneath: minimum 2,200 mm clearance.
- Overhead gantry signs: height per structural design, not less than the applicable vehicle height restriction on the road.

**Section 4(c) — Sight distance:**
- Signs must be visible for the required advance sight distance based on road speed.
- No tree, structure, or other sign may obstruct the sight line to a regulatory or warning sign.

## Sign Force and Precedence

1. A police officer's directive overrides all signs.
2. Traffic signals (Part 7) override static signs at the same location, except when a police officer is directing.
3. Temporary work zone signs (Part 9) override the corresponding permanent signs within the work zone.
4. An upright sign overrides an equivalent road surface marking where they conflict.
5. Specific signs override general signs (e.g., a lane-specific overhead sign overrides a roadside sign for that lane).

## Illuminated (פ) Signs

Self-luminous signs (פ prefix) are legally equivalent to their base sign counterparts. The illumination requirement applies in:
- Night-time conditions
- Any conditions of reduced visibility where retroreflective signs may not provide adequate conspicuity
- Locations specified in the sign board (e.g., tunnel approaches, school crossings)

## Ordinance Jurisdiction

The sign board applies to all roads in Israel open to public traffic (כבישים ציבוריים). Roads on private land may use signs from this board but are not required to do so unless the road is designated a public road by the relevant authority.

Enforcement authority: Israel Police (משטרת ישראל) and Local Authorities for urban roads.
Regulatory authority: Ministry of Transport (משרד התחבורה).

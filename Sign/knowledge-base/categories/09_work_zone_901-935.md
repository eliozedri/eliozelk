# Part 9: Work Zone Signs (901–935)

**Hebrew:** תמרורים בדרך בהדרך
**Sign range:** 901–935 (including illuminated variants פ901, פ933, פ934)
**Shape/Color:** Same shapes as equivalent base signs, but with **orange/amber** background or markings instead of standard colours
**General rule:** Work zone signs have the same legal force as standard equivalent signs but are used exclusively in and around roadworks areas. They take precedence within the work zone. Orange colouring distinguishes them from permanent signs.

---

| **Image** | `../../images/sign_901.png` |
